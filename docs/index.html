Getting started

We've finally added some docs. We don't have too much to say, it fits on one page! Anyways. You are here to learn how to use our oroducts to the best of their abilities. So let's get started.

For cases

General case instructions and guidelines.

  1. Make sure that you have the correct product.
  2. Make sure before you start assembling cases that everything is there and the correct color and grade.
  3. Check for any damages and contact us immediately.
  4. If everything is fine, keep your purchase information, warranties aren't applicable without purchase confirmation.
  5. Assemble according to the instructions. It doesn't matter if you've assembled cases 100 times or 1 time, follow the instructions.
  6. Have fun with the settings and features of your custom case.

For accessories

General instructions and guidelines.

  1. Make sure that you have the correct product.
  2. Make sure that everything is there and the correct color and product.
  3. Check for any damages and contact us immediately.
  4. If everything is fine, keep your purchase information, warranties aren't applicable without purchase confirmation.
  5. Enjoy the product!

For merch and apparel

General instructions and guidelines.

  1. Make sure that you have the correct product.
  2. Make sure that everything is there and the correct color and product.
  3. Check for any damages and contact us immediately.
  4. If you've bought the wrong size or product contact the store immediately.
  5. We don't give warranties for clothing but be sure to keep your purchase information still, it's a good habit.
  6. Enjoy your brand new quality, super comfortable and durable apparel!
